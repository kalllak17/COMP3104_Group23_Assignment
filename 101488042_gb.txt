George Brown College (GBC) 
George Brown College is a public institution in downtown Toronto with three main campuses: Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront. It emphasizes applied, hands-on learning with strong industry partnerships that support co-ops and field placements.
GBC is known for programs in Computer Technology, Construction & Engineering, Hospitality & Culinary Arts, Business, and Health Sciences.
Student Services (Highlights)
- Tutoring and learning centres
- Career services and co-op/field placement support
- Libraries, counselling, and student success hubs
GBC offers 175 full-time programs (including degree programs) and 182 continuing education certificate programs.
