Basic Overview

Full name: George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology. 

Founded: 1967, pursuant to Ontario’s college system reforms. 

Type: Public college. 


Strengths & Unique Features

GBC is very focused on experiential learning / work‑integrated learning (co‑ops, field placements, labs, etc.) so students have industry exposure. 

It works closely with employers to ensure its programs stay relevant and graduates are ready for the workforce. 

They are investing in sustainable infrastructure; e.g. a new building “Limberlost Place” which is net‑zero, mass timber, and using deep‑lake water cooling, etc.
